Why British Columbia?

From Sea to Sky

Dip your toes in the Pacific Ocean, roam through valley vineyards, climb to the tip of a mountian. You can do it all in BC. Known for it's many ski resorts, prime surfing spots, and impressive mountian ranges, BC is the perfect destination for the outdoor enthusist.

Take a break from the outdoors and settle into some of the finest city life Canada has to offer. Vancouver, Victoria, and Kelowna all offer unique experiences. Check out flower beds and musuems in Victoria, go club hopping in Vancouver, and take in all the oakanogan has to offer in Kelowna.

Whistler Blackcomb

Whistler blackcomb ski resort has more to offer than just skiing. World class resturants, hiking, swiming, and the peak-2-peak gondola offer plenty of entertainment all year round.

Okanagan Valley

The Okanagan Valley is known for it's warm summers and impressive wineries. Taste your way through BC's best wines and finish your day in downtown Kelowna.

Why Visit?

Tofino

Tofino is known for it's great surfing and laid-back vibes. Camp near Long Beach for leisurily walks or catch a wave in Cox Bay. You'll love Tofino and the people who call it home.

Surfing, camping, hiking, beaches, cafes.
